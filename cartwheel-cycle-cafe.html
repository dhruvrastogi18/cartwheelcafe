Our Story

Built for slowing down

The Cartwheel sits right in the heart of Ballymore Eustace — the sort of place cyclists and walkers pull into for a breather, and locals stop by out of habit.

Inside, shelves of second-hand books share the room with crafts made by artists from around the area. It's a small space with a lot going on: something to read, something to buy, something warm to eat, and no rush to finish any of it.

What's On Offer

Three good reasons to pull in

Coffee & Bakes

Good coffee and something sweet, made for lingering over a chat.

Simple Lunches

Honest, unfussy food to eat in or take with you on the road.

Books & Local Crafts

A small shop-within-a-shop — second-hand books and work by local makers.
